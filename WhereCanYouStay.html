Where can you stay?


Hotel Continental

Located in the heart of Timisoara, Continental Hotel is next to the Civic Park and only a few minutes' walk from the historical Unirii Square and the Bastion. Housed in one of the tallest buildings in the city, all rooms offer impressive views. The hotel boasts an in-house night club, spa and wellness centre, and a casino. The outdoor swimming pool and terrace overlook the Civic Park. Further facilities include an indoor swimming pool with a bar, a sauna and hot tubs. The 1,000 m² fitness centre offers guests modern amenities and aerobic-workout rooms.

- Along with a high level of experience in hospitality, we always keep focus on each detail in order to provide you the best quality services. The modern utilities, the luxury of the 164 rooms and the delicious cuisine, are only a few things that you will find in our hotel.

- Additionally, the hotel offers a restaurant serving Romanian and European dishes, including steak and seafood. A lobby bar and a beauty salon are also available on site.

- Continental Hotel is provided with a modern conference centre with fully equipped meeting rooms. The Traian Vuia International Airport is 10 km away. Airport shuttle transfers can be arranged at a surcharge and upon availability.

- Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 9.1 for a two-person trip.

- We speak your language!

Hotel Excelsior

Hotel Excelsior is a 10-minute walk from the historic pedestrian area of Timisoara, which offers numerous shopping opportunities. Timisoara Airport is a 15-minute drive away.

Offering light-coloured and spacious rooms, Hotel Excelsior features contemporary-style furniture and décor made by local artists.

- The refined Excelsior Restaurant offers gourmet dishes inspired by regional and international recipes, served in an intimate and pleasant ambience.

- Guests can unwind in the fitness centre of the Excelsior, or sip a refreshing drink while enjoying a massage.

- We speak your language!

Hotel Silva

Located a 10-minute walk from the centre of Timisoara and in the proximity of the main universities, Hotel Silva is a family-run hotel.

It features air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, a desk and flat-screen cable TV. The bathroom is stocked with free toiletries.

The services offered by the property include wake-up calls upon request, as well as cleaning and ironing at an extra charge. A 25-seats conference room can also be booked.

Read more:

- The restaurant menu offers a large variety of local and international meals, and you can also taste a selection of wines from the region. Private events can be organized upon request.

- The Liberty Square is 1.8 km away. You can reach Timisoara International Airport in approximately 30 minutes by car.

- Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.5 for a two-person trip.

- We speak your language!
